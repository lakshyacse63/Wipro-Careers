    Joining Wipro means embarking on a journey of continuous learning and growth. Our early career programs are designed to nurture young talent, providing you with the skills and experiences needed to thrive in a dynamic, global environment. Whether you’re looking for internships, apprenticeships, or entry-level positions, we offer a variety of opportunities to kickstart your career.

    Explore our early career programs and discover how you can start your journey with us. Your future begins here.​

    Law School Program

    The Legal and Compliance Team at Wipro actively recruits students from National Law Universities and other prestigious Law schools across India. Our Law School Program offers a clearly defined career path, allowing candidates to gain experience in areas such as corporate law, geography-specific compliance, and legal risk management. Managed closely by Wipro's General Counsel Office, this program provides law school graduates with diverse roles, fostering comprehensive career development.

    CA Hiring

    The Wipro Chartered Accountants Cohorts Program is a flagship two-year early careers initiative designed to nurture newly qualified Chartered Accountants into future-ready finance professionals. Offered as a full-time role within Wipro’s Corporate Finance function, the program combines structured learning with two rotational stints across key finance domains, enabling employees to build a strong foundation in business finance. Hiring is conducted through Campus Placements organized by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). Through hands-on experience, classroom-based learning, case studies, and continuous mentorship from senior finance leaders, the program develops technical expertise, business acumen, and leadership capabilities, preparing employees for long-term careers at Wipro.

    Elite

    Wipro's Technical Graduate Hiring Program seeks to attract the brightest and newly engineering graduates from across the country.

    Designed to identify and nurture high-potential talent, this program offers participants the chance to work on high-impact projects and gain invaluable industry exposure. Candidates receive hands-on training with cutting-edge technologies and engage in real-world client interactions, equipping them with the skills and experience needed to excel in their careers.

    Turbo​

    This technical hiring program is designed to identify and recruit top engineering talent from 46 premier institutes, including IIITs, NITs, and select top private colleges in India. It's the ideal launchpad for fresh engineers eager to kickstart their careers in a dynamic, learning-driven environment full of challenges and opportunities. Participants gain exposure to niche technologies, along with access to learning and certification programs, positioning them for success in the tech industry.

    STAR Hiring Program

    STAR Hiring Program is designed to fast-track deserving talent from India's top 13 technical institutes towards success. Through accelerated access to global client projects, participants gain early exposure to cutting-edge technologies alongside opportunities for learning, certification, and fully sponsored higher education. This program equips them with the skills and experience needed to lead in their careers.

    Wipro Integrated Learning Program (WILP)​

    WIMS (Wipro Infrastructure Management Services) and WASE (Wipro Academy of Software Excellence) are among Wipro’s flagship non-engineering campus hiring programs. Launched in 1995, Wipro pioneered this model 27 years ago, offering BCA and B.Sc. graduates a unique opportunity to gain both professional experience and higher education. Participants can work full-time at Wipro while pursuing a fully sponsored M.Tech degree from a prestigious institution, enjoying the dual benefits of career growth and academic advancement.

    B School Program

    We value new ideas and fresh perspectives from bright young minds. This philosophy at Wipro inspired the launch of our B-School Program in the 1980s. Over the years, this program has produced numerous leaders who have significantly shaped Wipro's vision. We recruit students pursuing 2-year full-time MBA degree from top business schools across India, offering them roles in critical areas such as Strategy, Finance, Consulting, and Human Resources. Participants undergo an extensive career development program that provides a comprehensive overview of our business, exposes them to it, and, more importantly, involves them in planning and executing our future growth strategies.
